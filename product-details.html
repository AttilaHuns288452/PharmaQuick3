Description

Paracetamol is a widely used over-the-counter pain reliever (analgesic) and fever reducer (antipyretic). It's commonly used to treat many conditions including headaches, body aches, toothaches, and fevers.

Dosage

Dosage & Administration 1 to 5 years old: 5 mL (1 teaspoonful) to 10 mL (2 teaspoonfuls); 6 to 12 years old: 10 mL (2 teaspoonfuls) to 20 mL (4 teaspoonfuls) to be taken 3 to 4 times a day. Or as prescribed by the physician.

Precautions

  • Do not use with other products containing paracetamol.
  • Do not exceed the recommended dose.
  • Consult a doctor if symptoms persist for more than 3 days.
  • Not recommended for children under 12 years without medical advice.
  • Avoid alcohol consumption while taking this medication.
