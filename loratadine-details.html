Loratadine 10mg Tablets

Allergy Relief

₱85.00 ₱95.00

Loratadine is a non-drowsy antihistamine that provides 24-hour relief from allergy symptoms such as sneezing, runny nose, itchy or watery eyes, and itchy throat or nose.

Description

Loratadine is a second-generation antihistamine that blocks the action of histamine, a substance in the body that causes allergic symptoms. Unlike first-generation antihistamines, Loratadine does not typically cause drowsiness when taken at recommended doses, making it suitable for daytime use. It provides effective relief from seasonal and year-round allergies with once-daily dosing.

Active Ingredient

Each tablet contains 10mg of Loratadine.

Uses

  • Temporary relief of symptoms due to hay fever or other upper respiratory allergies:
    • Runny nose
    • Sneezing
    • Itchy, watery eyes
    • Itching of the nose or throat
  • Relief of symptoms related to allergic skin conditions (such as hives)
  • Management of seasonal allergic rhinitis (hay fever)
  • Management of perennial allergic rhinitis (year-round allergies)

How It Works

Loratadine works by:

  • Blocking histamine receptors (H1 receptors) in the body
  • Preventing histamine from causing allergy symptoms
  • Reducing inflammation associated with allergic reactions
  • Providing relief without crossing the blood-brain barrier significantly, which helps minimize drowsiness

Dosage

Adults and children 12 years and over:

  • 1 tablet (10mg) once daily with a glass of water
  • Can be taken with or without food

Children 6 to under 12 years of age:

  • 1/2 tablet (5mg) once daily with a glass of water
  • Can be taken with or without food

Children under 6 years of age:

  • Consult a doctor before use

Adults 65 years and older:

  • No special dosage adjustment is required for elderly patients with normal kidney function

Precautions

Do not use if you have:

  • Ever had an allergic reaction to this product or any of its ingredients
  • Severe liver problems

Ask a doctor before use if you have:

  • Liver or kidney disease
  • Phenylketonuria (PKU) - some formulations may contain phenylalanine

When using this product:

  • Do not take more than directed
  • Avoid alcoholic drinks
  • Be careful when driving a motor vehicle or operating machinery

Drug Interactions: Loratadine may interact with:

  • Ketoconazole (antifungal medication)
  • Erythromycin (antibiotic)
  • Cimetidine (heartburn medication)
  • Other medications that may cause drowsiness

Possible Side Effects

Common side effects:

  • Headache
  • Fatigue
  • Dry mouth
  • Nervousness
  • Stomach pain

Less common side effects:

  • Dizziness
  • Drowsiness (uncommon at recommended doses)
  • Rapid heartbeat
  • Rash
  • Hair loss (rare)

Stop use and seek medical help if:

  • An allergic reaction occurs (rash, hives, itching, swelling of face, lips, tongue, or throat)
  • Symptoms do not improve within 7 days or are accompanied by fever
  • You experience unusual nervousness, dizziness, or sleeplessness

Storage

  • Store at room temperature (20-25°C or 68-77°F)
  • Protect from light and moisture
  • Keep the container tightly closed
  • Keep out of reach of children

Allergy Management Tips

  • Identify and avoid your allergy triggers when possible
  • Keep windows closed during high pollen seasons
  • Use air purifiers with HEPA filters in your home
  • Shower and change clothes after spending time outdoors during allergy season
  • Wash bedding regularly in hot water to reduce allergens
  • Consider wearing a mask when doing yard work or when pollen counts are high

When to See a Doctor

While Loratadine can effectively manage many allergy symptoms, you should consult a healthcare provider if:

  • Your symptoms are severe or do not improve after 7 days of treatment
  • You experience difficulty breathing or wheezing
  • You have symptoms of a severe allergic reaction (anaphylaxis)
  • Your allergies significantly impact your quality of life despite treatment
  • You need to take Loratadine for more than 6 months continuously
