About PharmaQuick

Learn more about our mission and project

Our Mission

PharmaQuick is dedicated to providing a comprehensive resource for over-the-counter (OTC) medicine products. Our mission is to educate and empower consumers in selecting appropriate OTC medicines by offering clear usage guidelines and dosage recommendations for each product.

We believe that informed decision-making leads to better health outcomes. By creating an intuitive platform with detailed product information and an effective filtering system, we aim to help users quickly identify the right OTC medicine for their specific needs.

Project Overview

This project was developed as part of the requirements for the Web Systems and Technologies course at the University of Caloocan City - Congressional Campus. It represents a practical application of web development skills to create a user-friendly platform that serves a genuine public need.

The website focuses exclusively on over-the-counter (OTC) medicine products, providing detailed listings that include typical usage and dosage recommendations. Our intuitive interface features a filtering system based on common ailments such as cold, cough, fever, and pain relief, making it easier for users to find the products they need.

Important Limitations

  • PharmaQuick excludes prescription drugs, as these require a doctor's authorization.
  • The information provided is for general guidance only and does not replace professional medical advice.
  • Users are encouraged to consult healthcare professionals for specific medical concerns or conditions.
