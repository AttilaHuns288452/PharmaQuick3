Aluminum Hydroxide & Magnesium Hydroxide Suspension

Digestive Health

₱75.00 ₱85.00

Neutralizes stomach acid and provides fast relief from heartburn, acid indigestion, and upset stomach. This dual-action formula combines aluminum hydroxide and magnesium hydroxide for effective acid neutralization.

Description

This antacid suspension contains a combination of aluminum hydroxide and magnesium hydroxide, which work together to neutralize excess stomach acid. The liquid suspension format allows for fast-acting relief of acid-related discomfort. It helps relieve symptoms associated with acid indigestion, heartburn, and upset stomach. The balanced formula minimizes the potential side effects that can occur with single-ingredient antacids.

Active Ingredients

  • Aluminum Hydroxide: Neutralizes stomach acid and helps reduce phosphate absorption
  • Magnesium Hydroxide: Neutralizes stomach acid and may have a mild laxative effect

Uses

  • Relief of heartburn and acid indigestion
  • Temporary relief of upset stomach associated with these symptoms
  • Acid reflux management
  • Relief from sour stomach
  • Temporary relief from discomfort due to excess stomach acid

Dosage

Adults and children 12 years and over: 2-4 teaspoons (10-20 mL) between meals and at bedtime, or as directed by a doctor.
Children under 12 years: Consult a doctor before use.

Shake well before use. May be taken with water.

How It Works

Antacids work by neutralizing stomach acid, which can provide quick relief from heartburn and indigestion. When you consume an antacid:

  1. The active ingredients (aluminum hydroxide and magnesium hydroxide) react chemically with stomach acid (hydrochloric acid)
  2. This reaction neutralizes the acid, raising the pH in your stomach
  3. As the acidity decreases, the burning sensation and discomfort are reduced
  4. The combination of ingredients provides balanced relief while minimizing side effects

Precautions

  • Do not use if you have kidney disease, except under the advice and supervision of a doctor
  • Ask a doctor or pharmacist before use if you are taking a prescription drug, as antacids may interact with certain medications
  • Stop use and ask a doctor if symptoms last more than 2 weeks
  • If you have kidney disease, consult with a healthcare provider before using products containing magnesium
  • Aluminum-containing antacids may cause constipation, while magnesium-containing antacids may cause diarrhea
  • Do not take the maximum dosage for more than 2 weeks except under the advice and supervision of a doctor

Storage

  • Store at room temperature (20-25°C or 68-77°F)
  • Keep tightly closed
  • Protect from excessive heat and moisture
  • Keep out of reach of children

When to Seek Medical Attention

While antacids can provide relief for occasional heartburn and indigestion, certain symptoms may indicate a more serious condition. Seek medical attention if you experience:

  • Severe or persistent abdominal pain
  • Difficulty swallowing
  • Frequent vomiting
  • Vomiting blood or material that looks like coffee grounds
  • Black, tarry stools
  • Unexplained weight loss
  • Chest pain that may be accompanied by shortness of breath, sweating, or pain radiating to the jaw, neck, or arm
