Electrolyte Water

Vitamins and Supplements

₱85.00 ₱95.00

Rehydrates and replenishes lost electrolytes to support hydration and energy. Ideal for active lifestyles and recovery.

Description

Electrolyte water is a hydration solution that contains essential minerals like sodium, potassium, calcium, and magnesium. These minerals, known as electrolytes, help regulate fluid balance, muscle function, and nerve signaling in the body. Electrolyte water is particularly beneficial during and after physical activity, illness, or exposure to heat when the body loses electrolytes through sweat.

Usage

Drink as needed to maintain hydration, especially during or after physical activity, illness with fever, vomiting or diarrhea, or exposure to hot weather. For athletes, consume before, during, and after intense exercise to maintain optimal hydration and performance.

Benefits

  • Replenishes essential electrolytes lost through sweat and physical activity
  • Supports proper muscle function and prevents cramping
  • Helps maintain proper hydration more effectively than plain water
  • Supports recovery after exercise or illness
  • Promotes optimal cellular function and energy production
