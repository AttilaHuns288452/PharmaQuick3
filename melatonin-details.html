Melatonin 10mg Tablets

Sleep Aid

₱280.00 ₱320.00

Melatonin is a natural sleep aid that helps regulate sleep-wake cycles. This supplement may reduce the time it takes to fall asleep and improve overall sleep quality for those with occasional sleeplessness.

Description

Melatonin is a hormone naturally produced by the pineal gland in the brain that helps regulate your body's sleep-wake cycle (circadian rhythm). As a supplement, melatonin can help address certain sleep problems, such as difficulty falling asleep, staying asleep, or adjusting to shift work. It may also help reduce the effects of jet lag when traveling across multiple time zones.

Our Melatonin 10mg tablets are designed to help you fall asleep faster and improve sleep quality without causing the morning grogginess often associated with prescription sleep medications. This non-habit-forming supplement supports a natural sleep cycle and can be particularly helpful for those experiencing occasional sleeplessness.

Active Ingredient

Each tablet contains 3mg of Melatonin.

Uses

  • Helps reduce the time it takes to fall asleep
  • May improve overall sleep quality
  • Helps establish normal sleep patterns
  • May help with jet lag symptoms when traveling across time zones
  • Can assist with sleep difficulties related to shift work
  • May help regulate sleep patterns in people with delayed sleep phase syndrome

How It Works

Melatonin works by:

  • Mimicking the effects of the natural hormone melatonin produced by your body
  • Binding to melatonin receptors in your brain, helping to regulate your sleep-wake cycle
  • Signaling to your body that it's time to sleep
  • Potentially reducing the time it takes to fall asleep
  • Helping to reset your body's internal clock when it's been disrupted

Dosage

Adults:

  • Take one tablet (3mg) 30-60 minutes before bedtime
  • Swallow tablet whole with water
  • Do not exceed the recommended dose unless directed by a healthcare professional

For Jet Lag:

  • Take one tablet 30-60 minutes before bedtime at your destination
  • Continue for 2-5 days as needed

Children:

  • Not recommended for children under 18 years without medical supervision

Precautions

Do not use if you have:

  • An autoimmune disorder
  • A seizure disorder
  • Depression
  • Diabetes
  • High blood pressure
  • Bleeding disorders

Consult a healthcare professional before use if you:

  • Are pregnant or breastfeeding
  • Have any medical conditions
  • Are taking any medications, especially:
    • Blood thinners (anticoagulants)
    • Immunosuppressants
    • Diabetes medications
    • Birth control pills
    • Sedatives or sleep medications
  • Are planning to have surgery
  • Are over 65 years of age

When using this product:

  • Avoid driving or operating machinery for 4-5 hours after taking melatonin
  • Avoid alcohol, as it may increase drowsiness and reduce effectiveness
  • Do not take with other sleep medications unless directed by a healthcare professional

Possible Side Effects

Common side effects:

  • Headache
  • Dizziness
  • Nausea
  • Drowsiness
  • Vivid dreams or nightmares

Less common side effects:

  • Short-term feelings of depression
  • Mild tremor
  • Mild anxiety
  • Irritability
  • Reduced alertness
  • Confusion or disorientation
  • Abdominal cramps

Stop use and consult a healthcare professional if:

  • You experience any severe or persistent side effects
  • Your sleep problems worsen or continue for more than 2 weeks
  • You experience new or worsening symptoms

Storage

  • Store at room temperature (20-25°C or 68-77°F)
  • Keep away from moisture, heat, and light
  • Keep the container tightly closed
  • Keep out of reach of children

Sleep Hygiene Tips

For best results with melatonin, combine with good sleep habits:

  • Maintain a regular sleep schedule (go to bed and wake up at the same time daily)
  • Create a relaxing bedtime routine
  • Keep your bedroom cool, dark, and quiet
  • Avoid screens (phones, tablets, computers) for at least 1 hour before bedtime
  • Avoid caffeine, alcohol, and large meals close to bedtime
  • Exercise regularly, but not too close to bedtime
  • Limit daytime naps to 30 minutes or less
  • Manage stress through relaxation techniques like deep breathing or meditation

Long-term Use

Melatonin is generally considered safe for short-term use. However, there is limited research on the long-term effects of melatonin supplementation. Consider the following:

  • Use melatonin for short periods (a few days to a few weeks) when needed
  • If sleep problems persist beyond 2 weeks, consult a healthcare professional
  • Avoid becoming dependent on any sleep aid, including melatonin
  • Address underlying causes of sleep problems when possible
  • Consider periodic breaks from melatonin if using regularly
