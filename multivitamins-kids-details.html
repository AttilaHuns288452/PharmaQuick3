Kids Multivitamin Gummies

Vitamins & Supplements

₱350.00 ₱395.00

Delicious fruit-flavored gummy vitamins specially formulated for growing children. Contains essential vitamins and minerals to support healthy growth, development, and immune function.

Description

These kid-friendly multivitamin gummies are designed to provide essential nutrients that support children's overall health and development. With a delicious fruit flavor that kids love, these gummies make daily vitamin supplementation easy and enjoyable. Each gummy is carefully formulated to provide a balanced blend of vitamins and minerals that growing bodies need, helping to fill nutritional gaps that may exist in a child's diet.

Key Ingredients

  • Vitamin A: Supports healthy vision and immune function
  • Vitamin C: Helps support immune health and acts as an antioxidant
  • Vitamin D3: Supports bone health and calcium absorption
  • Vitamin E: Acts as an antioxidant to protect cells
  • B Vitamins (B6, B12, Folate): Support energy metabolism and brain development
  • Zinc: Supports immune function and growth
  • Iodine: Supports thyroid function and brain development

Other Ingredients: Glucose syrup, sucrose, water, gelatin, natural flavors, natural colors (from fruits and vegetables), citric acid, lactic acid.

Benefits

  • Supports overall growth and development
  • Helps strengthen the immune system
  • Promotes healthy bone development
  • Supports brain function and cognitive development
  • Helps maintain healthy energy levels
  • Supports healthy vision
  • Delicious taste that kids enjoy
  • Easy to take - no pills to swallow
  • Free from artificial colors and flavors
  • No high fructose corn syrup

Recommended Usage

Children 2-3 years: One (1) gummy daily with food.

Children 4 years and older: Two (2) gummies daily with food.

For best results, have your child take the recommended dose consistently each day. Children should always chew the gummies thoroughly before swallowing.

Precautions

  • Keep out of reach of young children. These gummies look like candy and should be stored away from children's reach.
  • Do not exceed the recommended daily dose.
  • This product is not intended to replace a balanced diet.
  • Contains gelatin (not suitable for vegetarians).
  • If your child has any medical conditions or is taking medications, consult with a healthcare professional before use.
  • If your child has difficulty chewing or swallowing, this product may not be suitable.
  • Some children may experience mild digestive discomfort when first taking multivitamins.

Allergen Information

Free from:

  • Gluten
  • Dairy
  • Nuts
  • Soy
  • Egg
  • Artificial colors
  • Artificial flavors
  • High fructose corn syrup

Always check the ingredient list if your child has specific allergies or sensitivities.

Storage

  • Store in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight
  • Keep tightly closed
  • Do not refrigerate
  • Keep out of reach of children

Nutrition Tips for Children

While multivitamins can help fill nutritional gaps, they should complement a balanced diet. Here are some tips for supporting your child's nutrition:

  • Offer a variety of colorful fruits and vegetables daily
  • Include protein sources like lean meats, eggs, beans, or tofu
  • Provide calcium-rich foods such as milk, yogurt, or fortified plant milks
  • Choose whole grains over refined grains when possible
  • Limit processed foods and added sugars
  • Ensure adequate hydration throughout the day
  • Be a good role model by practicing healthy eating habits yourself

When to Consult a Healthcare Professional

While multivitamins are generally safe for most children, you should consult with a pediatrician or healthcare provider if:

  • Your child has a chronic medical condition
  • Your child is taking prescription medications
  • Your child has a history of allergic reactions
  • You're concerned about your child's growth or development
  • Your child has a restricted diet due to allergies, medical conditions, or dietary choices
  • Your child experiences any unusual symptoms after taking the multivitamin
